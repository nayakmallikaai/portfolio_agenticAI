Metric Definitions
Context Precision — tickers_used_in_summary ÷ tickers_fetched_via_tool. High = analyst fetched only what it needed.
Context Recall — holdings_mentioned_in_summary ÷ total_holdings. High = analyst covered the whole portfolio.
Answer Relevance — financial signal word density in the summary (normalised 0–1).
Price Grounding — % of proposed trade prices within 2% of fetched live price (mirrors risk auditor rule).
Faithfulness — every ticker in proposed_trades had its price fetched via tool (no hallucinated tickers).
Guardrail — % of guardrail checks passed (ShouldReject + RiskApproved checks).
Tool Call — % of tool-call checks passed (ordering, count, specific ticker, off-topic suppression).
Retry — % of retry checks passed (convergence, exhaustion, max-retries gate).
